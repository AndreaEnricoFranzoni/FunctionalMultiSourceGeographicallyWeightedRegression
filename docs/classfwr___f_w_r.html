yfunctional matrix containing the response (nx1)
Xcfunctional matrix containing the stationary covariates (nxqc)
Wcfunctional diagonal matrix containing the functional stationary weights (nxn)
Rcpenalization matrix of the stationary covariates (diagonal block matrix containing the the scalar product within the second order derivatives of the functional regression coefficients basis. LcxLc)
omegafunctional sparse matrix containing the basis of the functional regression coefficients of the stationary covariates (qcxLc, row i-th contains zeros and the basis of the i-th stationary covariate, their position shifted of sum_i_0_to_i(Lc_i))
qcnumber of stationary covariates
